Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you travel.

Do I need an international driver's license?

No, you do not necessarily need one prior to your arrival! You can easily obtain a temporary Sri Lankan driving permit at the 24/7 office located in the Airport Lobby. It takes only 10 minutes and costs 50 USD. To apply, you must present your valid national driving license with at least 6 months of validity remaining. If your license is not in English, you must bring an English translation officially stamped by your embassy. Alternatively, if you already have an International Driving Permit (IDP), you can get it endorsed in Colombo during regular working days. Once sorted, you are fully ready to hit the road and experience the beautiful scenery of Sri Lanka!

Do I need to pay a security deposit for a car rental?

Yes, a refundable security deposit is required for all car rentals. The minimum deposit amount is $300 USD. This will be fully refunded to you at the end of your rental period, provided the vehicle is returned in its original condition.

How do I pay for the tour or rental?

Payment details will be discussed upon booking confirmation via WhatsApp. Usually, we accept cash payments (USD, EUR, or LKR) upon arrival at the airport or at the start of your tour.

Are there mileage limits on the cars?

No, our daily rates include unlimited mileage. You don't have to worry about hidden kilometer charges at the end of your trip.

Can you pick me up from Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB)?

Yes, absolutely. Since we are located in Katunayake, we are perfectly positioned to pick you up straight from the arrivals terminal at any time of day or night.

Can we customize the tour itineraries?

Yes! The tour packages listed on our website are our most popular routes, but we specialize in custom, tailor-made itineraries. Let us know what you want to see, and we will plan the perfect route.
